General info about ISA

  • Just 2500/- will be charged as registration fee for joining MAS, which is fully refundable only if you join MAS and remain unplaced till 30th June 2022
  • No ISA payments will be collected if placed below 6 lakh, We will be helping you to cross the bar
  • You will be signing our ISA once you are registered with us Once registered student also has an option to pay 28,999 / 5000 for 6 months upfront during preparation (May-Oct 2021)
  • If you choose to pay net ISA one at a time then you have to pay it within 15 days of your placement
  • You are entitled to share offer letter received from the company and other documents for assuring proper ISA transactions
  • If you are fired/left/switched the job before completion of ISA, your ISA will be paused and continued with you next job till completion. (Except you don’t wish to do job)
